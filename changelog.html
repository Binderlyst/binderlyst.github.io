Changelog

What's changed in Binderlyst, newest first.

1.0.21

13 July 2026

  • The scan result now follows the condition you pick. The price scales to that grade and shows in the grade's colour, so it's clear you're looking at a condition estimate rather than full market price.
  • Fixed product searches getting stuck once a filter had been applied. Typing in the search box, or clearing it, now updates the list straight away.
  • The keyboard's search key now applies product searches, the same as it already did for cards.

1.0.20

12 July 2026

  • New Custom look. Build your own theme in Settings: pick dark or light, start from any built-in look, then change any colour with a full colour picker or exact hex codes. Changes apply live, and the app warns you if your text would be hard to read. Free for everyone.
  • The look picker in Settings is now a swipeable row with a small check on the selected look. The Mystic look has been retired: if you had it selected you've been moved to Arcane, and the Custom look can recreate it if you miss it.
  • Set Completion is quicker when switching between sets, progress bars animate in as totals arrive, and totals are remembered for a week.
  • New installs start with a tidier homepage layout, with Latest Releases first. Existing layouts are untouched.
  • Prices can now link out to TCGplayer: tap a price to see that card there, and want lists have a Buy missing button for the cards you still need. There's a toggle in Settings if you'd rather not have the links, and they're always labelled "Affiliate links, we may earn a commission."
  • There's now a Binderlyst Patreon for anyone who'd like to chip in. Donations only, no in-app perks, with a small info page in the app.
  • New installs now start with a sensible default currency, plus a few other small fixes, including a button for leaving a Play Store review and the scanner screen layout on some phones.

1.0.16

9 July 2026

  • Continued improvements to want lists.
  • Small fixes and polish.

1.0.13

2 July 2026

  • A round of crash and data-loss fixes. Restoring a backup now checks it before touching anything on your device, saves can no longer bring back deleted cards, and several startup and scanner crashes are gone.
  • Decks: a format-aware deck builder (Commander, Standard and more) with legality checks, built around the cards you own.
  • Want lists: keep lists of the cards you're after.
  • Rapid batch scanning: confident scans are added automatically, the rest wait in a review tray.
  • Card conditions: grade each copy across seven grades, with optional prices that scale to the grade.
  • The scanner now detects a card's language automatically.
  • Opt-in community stats: if you like, share anonymised collection stats to help build community numbers.
  • The app quietly started recording your collection's value each day, ready for value history later.
